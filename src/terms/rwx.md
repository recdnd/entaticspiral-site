# RWX

## Definition
RWX is the permission model governing read, write, and execution rights in Spiral structures.

## In Spiral
- Controls access without implying authority.
- Operates under sovereignty constraints.
- Applies to modules and artifacts.

## Structure
Permissions are expressed as explicit flags bound to entities.

## Notes
RWX alone cannot override sealed fragments.
