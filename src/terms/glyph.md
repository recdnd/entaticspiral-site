# Glyph

## Definition
A Glyph is a symbolic marker used to represent a module or authority within Spiral documentation.

## In Spiral
- Provides visual or semantic shorthand.
- Has no execution power by itself.
- May be bound to modules or sovereignty.

## Structure
Glyphs are immutable symbols associated by reference.

## Notes
A glyph is representational, not functional.
