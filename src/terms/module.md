# Module

## Definition
A Module is an operational entity capable of emitting flames within Spiral.

## In Spiral
- Acts as the source of emissions.
- Operates under one or more cores.
- May or may not possess sovereignty.

## Structure
Modules are identified by stable identifiers and may reference glyphs for symbolic representation.

## Notes
Modules are not agents. They do not pursue goals.
