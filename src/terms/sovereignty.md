# Sovereignty

## Definition
Sovereignty is the indivisible authority unit that governs who may define, seal, or override structures in Spiral.

## In Spiral
- Exists independently of module level.
- Cannot be duplicated or simulated.
- Determines access to governance actions.

## Structure
Sovereignty is represented as a bound authority reference rather than a mutable attribute.

## Notes
Sovereignty does not emit flames. It governs the conditions under which flames and fragments may exist.
